    OUR PACKAGE

    Museum Sepuluh November

    November 10 Museum to see historical relics during the colonial period. This monument was built to commemorate the events of the November 10 battle in Surabaya. At that time there were a lot of Sorboyo areks who died fighting the Dutch.

    Museum Dr. Soetomo

    Dr. Soetomo Museum is a special museum that displays the life history of Dr Soetomo, a movement figure and one of the founders of the Boedi Oetomo organization.

    Museum Pendidikan Surabaya

    The Surabaya Education Museum is a thematic museum which was established as a step to preserve the nation's history and culture with the aim of supporting educational, research and recreational activities in the city of Surabaya. This museum stores material evidence of education during the Pre-Literate period, the Classic period, the Colonial period and the Independence period.

    Museum H.O.S. Tjokroaminoto

    The HOS Tjokroaminoto Museum is the home of HOS Tjokroaminoto. This museum has high historical value because it is often used as a meeting place for nationalist leaders. They spent hours discussing ideologies and strategies to achieve national independence.

    Museum Surabaya (Gedung Siola)

    The Surabaya Museum is a government museum that provides information about the history of the city of Surabaya. The Surabaya Siola Museum is said to have more than 1,000 collections. Almost all of them have an important role in the history of the development of the City of Heroes.

    Museum W.R. Soepratman

    W. R. Soepratman's house is the house where the creator of the song Indonesia Raya died. The museum building was the house belonging to WR's older brother. Soepratman, whose name is Roekiyem Soepratijah. WR. Soepratman lived in the house in 1937, until he died on August 17 1938.
